About Me

As a former Navy veteran and police officer, I've had the opportunity to serve and protect my community in a variety of ways. Now, I'm excited to take on a new challenge as a full-stack software developer.

Throughout my career, I've learned the importance of attention to detail, adaptability, and a strong work ethic. These skills have carried over into my passion for coding, where I enjoy taking on complex problems and finding elegant solutions.

My experience in law enforcement has taught me the value of teamwork, communication, and being able to think on my feet. These are all qualities that I bring to my work in software development, where collaboration and clear communication are key.

I'm excited about the opportunities that the tech industry has to offer and am eager to continue learning and growing in my career. With a dedication to excellence and a willingness to take on new challenges, I know that I have what it takes to succeed in this exciting and ever-changing field.
